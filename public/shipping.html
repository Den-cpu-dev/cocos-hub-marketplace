Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We offer standard shipping across Ghana. Delivery times vary based on your location:

  • Accra & Tema: 1-2 business days
  • Other Regional Capitals: 3-5 business days
  • Rural Areas: 5-7 business days

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address. We offer FREE shipping on orders above GH₵1000.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your item, you may return it within 7 days of delivery under the following conditions:

  • The item must be unused and in its original packaging.
  • For skincare and beauty products, the seal must remain intact due to hygiene reasons.
  • Bags and accessories must have all original tags attached.

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please contact our support team at support@cocoshub.com with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with further instructions on where to send your package.
