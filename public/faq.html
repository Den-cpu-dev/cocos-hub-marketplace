Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Accra?

Yes, we deliver to all regions in Ghana! Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. We source all our skincare and bags directly from authorized distributors and reputable brands to ensure 100% authenticity.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation message with a tracking number (if available) or a delivery partner contact info.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Mobile Money (MTN, Telecel, AT), and Bank Transfers. We are working on adding direct card payments soon.

Can I change my order after placing it?

If you need to change your order, please contact us immediately. Once an order is processed for shipping, we can no longer make changes.
